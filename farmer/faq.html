Frequently Asked Questions

How does the supply chain system work?
Our supply chain system connects farmers directly with buyers, ensuring transparency, fair pricing, and efficiency in transactions.
What kind of crops and products can I sell here?
You can sell fresh fruits, vegetables, grains, dairy products, organic produce, and other farm-related items. If you are unsure about a product, check the "Allowed Items" section or contact support.
How can farmers list their products?
Farmers can list products through our platform by these three steps-
  1. Login to your account.
  2. Go to inventory section(first option on the side navbar).
  3. Upload all details(choose your product category, set pricing, etc.)
Is this website is multilingual?
Yes, You can use this website in your desired language.
What happens when a consumer places an order?
You will receive a notification with order details. You can accept or reject the order within a given timeframe. Once accepted, you need to prepare the order for delivery.
How and when will I receive my payment?
Payments are processed securely through bank transfers or UPI. You will receive payments within 3-4 working days after order completion, depending on the selected payment cycle.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept online payments, UPI, bank transfers, and cash on delivery in selected regions.
How is quality assurance handled?
All products go through strict quality checks before delivery to ensure high standards.
